Andrey Starostin
22 October 1906 - 24 October 1987

Starostin family produced four legendary football figures - brothers Nikolay, Alexander, Pyotr and Andrey himself.

Andrey Starostin took half-back and defender positions on the football field and started representing Moscow football clubs at the age of 16.

Along with his brothers, Andrey Starostin was one of the founders of the Spartak football club (1935). He played for Spartak himself from 1935 to 1942 (1937 – 1940 as a captain).

Starostin became a multiple champion and prize-winner of RSFSR and USSR championships (1931, 1935, 1936, 1937, 1938, 1939, 1940) and the USSR Cup (1938, 1939).
